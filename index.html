About the Initiative

A Bridge Between Citizens & Governance

Core Mission Statement
Parent Organization
Parivartan Sarathi Foundation
Led by Mr. Tejas Pujari, Project Lead

Project Postman is a citizen-driven public grievance, accountability, governance support, and civic-policy initiative created to bridge the gap between people and the system.

The initiative enables citizens to raise issues related to governance, corruption, public services, administration, policy implementation, civic infrastructure, institutional negligence, and public accountability.

Project Postman works as a structured civic-action mechanism that follows issues from complaint stage to resolution stage — through research, official communication, RTI processes, administrative escalation, policy recommendations, and legal pathways wherever necessary.

We believe that public grievances should not remain limited to complaints alone. Every issue carries insights that can help improve governance systems, administrative efficiency, and public policy — shaping a stronger, more accountable democracy.

How We Work

A Structured Path from Grievance to Resolution

Every complaint that enters our system follows a rigorous, transparent, multi-stage process — ensuring nothing is buried, forgotten, or ignored.

The People Citizens who refused to stay silent.
The System The institutions that must listen.

Entry Stage

Citizens Post Problems

Citizens submit complaints, public grievances, corruption concerns, policy failures, and administrative negligence through the Project Postman platform and Digital Complaint Box.

Civic IssuesCorruptionRTIInfrastructure
2
Analysis Stage

Review, Study & Documentation

The team reviews submitted information, studies the issue in detail, conducts policy and legal analysis, and identifies possible administrative and legal pathways.

Policy AnalysisLegal ResearchEvidence Review
3
Action Stage

Direct Authority Follow-Up

Project Postman directly follows up with local authorities, municipal bodies, district administration, government departments, state and central ministries.

Municipal BodiesState MinistriesCentral Govt.
4
Transparency Stage

RTI & Information Gathering

Where information is hidden or delayed, Project Postman files RTI applications, seeks official records, and conducts fact verification for evidence-based civic action.

RTI ApplicationsPublic RecordsFact Verification
5
Reform Stage

Policy Research & Recommendations

Based on complaint analysis and RTI findings, Project Postman prepares issue-based research reports and drafts policy recommendations for submission to concerned departments.

Research ReportsPolicy DraftsReform Frameworks
6
Escalation Stage

Systematic Complaint Escalation

If action is not taken at one level, the matter is escalated through departmental complaints, state ministry representations, and central ministry escalation.

Multi-level EscalationAccountability
7
Communication Stage

Monitoring & Citizen Updates

Project Postman continuously monitors every active case. Complainants receive regular updates regarding complaint status, authority responses, and RTI progress.

Real-time UpdatesTransparency

Legal Escalation

If no meaningful action is taken, the matter may proceed through legal channels — including legal notices, tribunal complaints, court proceedings, and public interest legal action.

Impact Stories

Voices That Changed
the System.

Real stories of citizens who raised their voice — and the system that had to listen.

Infrastructure

The Road That Was Ignored for Three Years

A pothole-riddled road in Bhandara left two-wheelers damaged and families unable to reach hospitals at night. After filing through DCB, the Municipal Council responded within 21 days.

Bhandara, Maharashtra DCB · Infrastructure
Education

Students Left Without Scholarships for Two Semesters

Dozens of SC/OBC students at a Nagpur college hadn't received state scholarship disbursements for eight months. An RTI filing by Project Postman uncovered administrative misrouting of funds.

Nagpur, Maharashtra RTI · Education
Water & Sanitation

A Village That Hadn't Seen Clean Water in 60 Days

A rural cluster in Yavatmal district went two months without tap water supply due to a broken pump. Repeated verbal complaints to the Gram Panchayat were dismissed — until documentation forced action.

Yavatmal, Maharashtra DCB · Water & Sanitation
Corruption

Funds Sanctioned, Construction Never Began

A community hall construction project was approved and fully funded two years prior — yet not a single brick was laid. A formal RTI application revealed fund diversion; the case was escalated to the district collector.

Wardha, Maharashtra RTI · Anti-Corruption
Healthcare

A Public Hospital With No Doctor on Duty for Weeks

Patients at a primary health centre in a tribal belt were turned away for over three weeks with staff absent. The complaint, filed with supporting photographic evidence, was forwarded to the Civil Surgeon's office.

Gadchiroli, Maharashtra DCB · Healthcare
Policy Reform

A Student's Suggestion That Became a Policy Draft

A law student submitted a detailed proposal through the DSB recommending a local grievance redressal timeline mandate for municipal offices. The proposal was compiled into a formal governance reform submission.

Pune, Maharashtra DSB · Policy Reform

Vision & Mission

A Citizen-First Governance Ecosystem

To build a transparent, accountable, citizen-first governance ecosystem where people are heard, informed, protected, and empowered to seek solutions through structured civic action and democratic engagement.

  • Bridge citizens and governance systems effectively
  • Transform complaints into structured, documented action
  • Strengthen public accountability at every level
  • Promote transparency through RTI and information access
  • Support citizens through research and legal processes
  • Contribute toward policy and governance improvements
  • Create a stronger culture of democratic participation

Confidentiality & Complainant Protection

Protection of complainants is a core principle. Project Postman maintains strict confidentiality of complainant identity, whistleblower information, sensitive documents, and all personal details — ensuring the safety of citizens raising concerns against powerful institutions.
